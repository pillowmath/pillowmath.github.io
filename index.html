Math Tutoring

I am tutoring students at UC Berkeley in math, statistics, and computer science classes. I tutor in CS 70, Math 1A/B, Math 10A/B, Math 16A/B, Math 53, Math 54, Math 55, Math 104, Math 110, Math 113, Math 185, Stat 134, Stat 155, and more (including graduate classes).

If you are in the Los Angeles area, I may be able to meet you in person. Otherwise, we can do Skype tutoring sessions. My rate is $50/hr. If this really isn't something you can afford, then talk to me; we can see what works for you.

I am a PhD student in math at UCLA. I graduated from UC Berkeley with a BA in math and have several years of experience tutoring UC Berkeley students in math classes.

If you know anyone who is looking for a math tutor, please direct them to me. Thanks!
